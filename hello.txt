1、When all else is lost the future still remains.

就是失去了一切别的，也还有未来。

2、Keep on going never give up.

勇往直前， 决不放弃！

3、There is no garden without its weeds.

没有不长杂草的花园。

4、The best preparation for tomorrow is doing your best today.

对明天做好的准备就是今天做到最好！

5、Suffering is the most powerful teacher of life.

苦难是人生最伟大的老师。

6、A man can't ride your back unless it is bent.

你的腰不弯，别人就不能骑在你的背上。

7、The shortest way to do many things is to only one thing at a time.

做许多事情的捷径就是一次只做一件事。

8、Victory won''t come to me unless I go to it.

胜利是不会向我们走来的，我必须自己走向胜利。
